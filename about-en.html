Ministry of Development

A history of decades serving the Greek economy and sustainable development.

History & Evolution

The Ministry of Development has a long history of shaping Greece's economic policy. Since its establishment, it has evolved to meet the changing needs of the Greek economy.

Today, the Ministry of Development is responsible for formulating and implementing economic development policies, promoting entrepreneurship, utilizing European resources, and attracting investments.

Based at Kanigos Square in Syntagma, the Ministry is staffed by hundreds of civil servants and operates in close cooperation with European and international organizations.

1974

Establishment of Ministry of National Economy

The original Ministry was established with responsibilities for national economic policy planning.

1982

Renamed to Ministry of National Economy

Integration of industry and energy responsibilities.

1995

Creation of General Secretariat for Investments

Systematic foreign investment attraction begins.

2004

Establishment of Ministry of Development

Official establishment with modern development responsibilities.

2014

ESPA 2014-2020

Management of €10.6 billion for the 2014-2020 programming period.

2021

ESPA 2021-2027

New programming period begins with increased resources.
