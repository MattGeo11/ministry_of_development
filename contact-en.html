Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for a subsidy program, first check the availability of the program on our website. Then, you will need to create an account in the application submission system and complete the online application with the required supporting documents.

The basic requirements include:

  • Be a registered business in Greece
  • The investment plan must exceed minimum cost thresholds depending on the sector
  • Environmental terms must be met
  • New jobs must be created
  • The plan must contribute to economic development

Evaluation time varies depending on the program and the volume of applications. Usually, evaluation takes 2-4 months from the application deadline. For the Development Law, the process can take up to 6 months.

Generally, you can submit applications for more than one program, but the same investment plan cannot be subsidized by two sources for the same expenses. Check the terms of each program for specific restrictions.

You can track the progress of your application through the application submission system using your account details. You will also receive email notifications at each stage of the evaluation.

Eligible expenses include:

  • Machinery and equipment
  • Buildings and facilities
  • Digital infrastructure and software
  • Energy upgrade
  • Payroll expenses (in some programs)
  • Technical services and consulting
