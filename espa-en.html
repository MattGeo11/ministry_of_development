What is ESPA

European Structural Funds

ESPA (European Structural Funds) is Greece's main financial instrument for promoting economic development and social cohesion. Through ESPA 2021-2027, significant resources are available to support businesses, innovation, and sustainability.

  • Funding for small and medium enterprises
  • Innovation and research support
  • Green transition support
  • Digital transformation
  • Job creation
  • Export orientation enhancement

Types of Investments

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

Attracting foreign investment in strategic sectors such as technology, energy, and tourism. One-stop services for investors.

Open Target: €5 billion annually

Strategic Investments

Investments over €5 million in strategically important sectors with special incentives and fast licensing procedures.

Open Minimum: €5 million

ESPA Investments

Funding from the European Structural Funds for investments in manufacturing, services, and tourism.

Open Support up to 65%

Development Law Investments

Incentives for investments contributing to economic development, employment, and regional convergence.

Open Multiple rounds
