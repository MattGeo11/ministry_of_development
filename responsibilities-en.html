Responsibilities

The areas of responsibility and key functions of the Ministry of Development.

Strategic Planning

Design and implementation of national development strategies for sustainable economic growth of the country. Coordination of policies with European and international organizations.

Competitiveness

Enhancing the competitiveness of the Greek economy through structural reforms, improvement of the business environment, and promotion of innovation.

Areas of Action

  • National development policy formulation
  • Public investment coordination
  • Macroeconomic indicators monitoring
  • Consultation with social partners
  • International economic relations

SME Support

Providing tools and resources for the establishment, operation, and growth of small and medium-sized enterprises. Support for new entrepreneurs and promotion of entrepreneurial culture.

Development Law

Management and implementation of the Development Law, providing incentives and subsidies for investment projects that contribute to economic development.

Support Actions

  • Subsidies and tax incentives
  • Consultancy programs
  • Business networking
  • Incubators and business parks
  • Export development

Technological Innovation

Promoting technological innovation as a driver of growth. Supporting research activities and collaborations between businesses and academic institutions.

Digital Transformation

Accelerating the digital transformation of businesses and the public sector. Support for the adoption of digital technologies.

Areas of Action

  • Research and Development (R&D)
  • Technology transfer
  • Digital skills
  • Startups and innovation ecosystems
  • Digital infrastructure

European Resources Management

Management and monitoring of ESPA 2021-2027 and other European programs. Ensuring effective absorption of available resources.

Investment Attraction

Attracting Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and promoting Greece as an attractive investment destination. Support for high-value investment projects.

Development Incentives

  • Development law investment incentives
  • Strategic investments
  • Tax and financial investment incentives
  • Technical infrastructure and industrial zones
  • One-stop services for investors

Business Internationalization

Supporting Greek businesses to enter international markets. Promoting Greek products and services abroad.

International Cooperation

Developing bilateral and multilateral economic relations. Participation in European and international forums for economic policy.

Export Actions

  • Export activities and missions
  • Trade agreements
  • Promotion of Greek investments abroad
  • Economic diplomacy
  • International business partnerships
